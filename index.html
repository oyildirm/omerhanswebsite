Creative Statement

My work is focused on the weirdness of the world, of the unusual but facsinating aspects of life that create mystery but asks the question of how. The inspiration of many of the works that I will produce are from the uncanny images online that are called liminal spaces. The emptiness of a world but can also feel familiarity. The quiet but the loud part of the world that is left in the dark. The brief image that can mean nothing to a person but also mean everything to them, that is what I want to accomplish.
